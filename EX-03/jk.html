Union Territory of Jammu and Kashmir

About Jammu and Kashmir

Jammu and Kashmir is a union territory in northern India, known for its stunning Himalayan landscapes, beautiful valleys, and rich cultural heritage.

Lieutenant Governor

The current Lieutenant Governor of Jammu and Kashmir is Manoj Sinha.

Population Details

  • Total Population: ~13 million (2023)
  • Summer Capital: Srinagar
  • Winter Capital: Jammu
  • Official Languages: Kashmiri, Dogri, Urdu, Hindi, English

Major Cities in Jammu and Kashmir

  • Srinagar
  • Jammu
  • Anantnag
  • Baramulla
  • Udhampur
