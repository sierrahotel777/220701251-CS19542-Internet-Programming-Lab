State of Tamil Nadu

About Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu is a state in southern India, known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and beautiful landscapes.

Chief Minister

The current Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu is M. K. Stalin.

Population Details

  • Total Population: ~72 million (2023)
  • Capital: Chennai
  • Official Language: Tamil

Major Cities in Tamil Nadu

  • Chennai
  • Coimbatore
  • Madurai
  • Trichy
  • Salem
