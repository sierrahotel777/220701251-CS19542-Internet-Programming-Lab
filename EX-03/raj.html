State of Rajasthan

About Rajasthan

Rajasthan is a state in northern India, known for its desert landscapes, palaces, and vibrant cultural heritage.

Chief Minister

The current Chief Minister of Rajasthan is Ashok Gehlot.

Population Details

  • Total Population: ~80 million (2023)
  • Capital: Jaipur
  • Official Language: Hindi

Major Cities in Rajasthan

  • Jaipur
  • Jodhpur
  • Udaipur
  • Kota
  • Bikaner
